Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ronim Studio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Skintel mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Selfie photos. When you take a photo for a skin analysis, the image is processed to generate your skin score and insights.
  • Account information. If you sign in (e.g. with Apple), we receive an identifier and, where you allow it, your email address.
  • Analysis results. Your skin scores and history are stored so you can track changes over time.
  • Purchase information. Subscription status is managed through the App Store / Google Play and our payments provider (RevenueCat). We do not receive or store your card details.
  • Device & usage data. Basic technical data (device type, app version, crash logs) used to keep the App working.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze your selfie and provide skin scores, insights, and recommendations.
  • To save your scan history within your account.
  • To manage your subscription and restore purchases.
  • To send optional scan reminders if you enable notifications.
  • To improve reliability and fix issues.

3. Your Photos

Your selfies are used only to produce your skin analysis. They are processed securely and associated with your account so you can view your history. We do not sell your photos, and we do not use them for advertising.

4. Data Storage & Processing

We use Supabase for secure authentication and data storage, and trusted AI/processing providers to analyze images. These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers that operate the App (hosting, authentication, image analysis, payments).
  • Authorities when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account data and scan history while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Contact).

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children

Skintel is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Medical Disclaimer

Skintel provides cosmetic and informational insights only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. Consult a qualified dermatologist for any skin concern.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions about this Policy or your data? Email us at ronimstudio.play@gmail.com.
